{{flexstart|class=section|id=main-section}}{{options/set|item|scale=0.75}}{{infocard|id=box-game|class=compact terraria|name=The Game|iconalt=[[File:Tree.png|x48px|link=]]|text=
''[[Terraria]]'' is a land of adventure! A land of mystery! A land that's yours to shape, defend, and enjoy. Your options in ''Terraria'' are limitless. Are you an action gamer with an itchy trigger finger? A master builder? A collector? An explorer? There's something for everyone. Start by building basic shelter, then dig for [[ore]] and other resources. Discover and [[Recipes|craft]] over 500 [[weapons]] of [[magic]], [[ranged]], [[melee]] and [[Summon weapons|summon]] varieties, as well as [[armor]], and use them to battle hundreds of different [[enemies]]. Soon you'll be going head-to-head with any of a dozen enormous [[bosses]]. Go [[fishing]], ride a [[mount]], find [[Floating Islands]], build [[house]]s for helpful [[NPCs]], and much, much more.
